Comeback Sauce

Slightly tangy, spicy mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together and chill briefly before use.
