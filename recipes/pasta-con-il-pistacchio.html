pasta con il pistacchio

pasta with pistacchios and sausage

Ingredients

  • 4 links sweet italian sausage
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 glass white wine
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • crushed red pepper flakes
  • 28oz whole peeled tomatoes, chopped or crushed by hand
  • a few mint leaves
  • 1 lb casarecce pasta
  • 1 cup crushed pistacchio, divided in two

Steps

Using two pans, one to saute the sausage, another for garlic:

  1. In the first pan, saute the sausage, casings removed, in olive oil and break up. When browned, add a glass of white wine and cook off.
  2. In the other pan, saute garlic and a bit of crushed red pepper. Once aromatic, add the chopped whole tomatoes and half of the pistacchio.
  3. Once the sauce is combined, allow to simmer for about 15 minutes. While sauce is simmering, cook pasta to package instructions.
  4. When simmering is done, combine sausage with sauce and salt to taste.
  5. With the fire off, add mint leaves and the rest of the pistacchios, then toss with pasta.
