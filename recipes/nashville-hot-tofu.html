nashville hot tofu

sacrilege

Ingredients

hot butter

  • 8 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp distilled vinegar
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp ground cayenne
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

tofu

  • 1 package firm tofu, drained
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup AP flour
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp baking pwder
  • kosher salt
  • heaping 1/2 cup chilled seltzer water / club soda
  • vegetable oil

assembly

  • 8 slider buns or quartered buns / bread
  • lettuce, pickles
  • mayonnaise or comeback sauce

Steps

  1. melt butter in a small saucepan and mix in remaining hot bbutter ingredients.
  2. Divide tofu into eight pieces by cuting the tofu in half parallel to the cutting board, then quartering each half.
  3. Pat tofu dry and set on towel or paper towel lined plate.
  4. Place milk and half of the flour in shallow bowls.
  5. Whisk half the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and salt together in a medium bowl, then gently whisk in soda water.
  6. Heat a half inch of oil in a heavy skillet to 375 degrees.
  7. Dredge tofu in milk, then completely coat in flour. Dip in batter, drip off excess, and cook in the oil in two batches.
  8. Fry undisturbed for at least 2 minutes to allow batter to set. Turn and cook for about 3 more minutes or until golden brown on all sides, flipping more as needed.
  9. Transfer cooked tofu to a paper towel-lined surface and sprinkle with salt.
  10. Brush tofu with hot tofu and serve on buns with desired toppings.
